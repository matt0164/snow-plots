Public Information Statement
National Weather Service Anchorage AK
521 PM AKST Sat Feb 8 2025

...Anchorage International Airport Climate Highlights for
January 2025...

...7th Warmest/66th Coldest January...
...4th Wettest/69th Driest January...

The period of record includes 72 Januarys.

*** Delineates 2025 in the lists below.

...Temperature...

January 2025 was the 7th warmest January on record with an
average temperature of 26.7 degrees. This is 9.8 degrees above
the normal of 16.9 degrees.

Top 5 Warmest Januarys By Average Temperature
---------------------------------------------
1) 32.0 degrees in 1977.
2) 31.5 degrees in 1981.
3) 30.3 degrees in 2014.
4) 30.2 degrees in 1985.
5) 27.4 degrees in 2001.


Top 5 Coldest Januarys By Average Temperature
---------------------------------------------
1)  2.7 degrees in 1971.
2)  2.9 degrees in 1973 2012.
3)  3.4 degrees in 1989.
4)  4.6 degrees in 1969.
5)  5.1 degrees in 1956.

The average high in January 2025 was 31.5 degrees, which was 8.8
degrees above the normal of 22.7 degrees. The average low was 22.0
degrees, which was 11.0 degrees above the normal of 11.0 degrees.

23 days in January had above normal temperatures. 8 days in
January had below normal temperatures. 0 days in January had
normal temperatures (within 1/2 degree).

9 days in January were 20 degrees or more above the daily normal.
19 days in January were 10 degrees or more above the daily normal.
22 days in January were 5 degrees or more above the daily normal.
6 days in January were 5 degrees or more below the daily normal.
4 days in January were 10 degrees or more below the daily normal.
0 days in January were 20 degrees or more below the daily normal.

The most above normal day in January occurred on the 12th and 24th,
which were 23.8 degrees warmer than the daily normal.

The most below normal day in January occurred on the 31st, which
was 17.1 degrees cooler than the daily normal.

The warmest daily high in January occurred on the 14th, when the
high reached 46 degrees. This ties 7 previous years, with the most
recent year 2015, for the 10th warmest monthly high in January.

Top 5 Warmest Monthly Highs in January
--------------------------------------
1) 50 degrees - 1961 2014.
2) 49 degrees - 1977 1985.
3) 48 degrees - 1994.
4) 47 degrees - 1960 1981 1986 2022.
5) 46 degrees - 1980 1991 1992 1997 2001 2011 2015 2025. ***

The coldest daily low in January occurred on the 31st, when the
low dropped to -4 degrees. This is the 58th coldest monthly low
in January.

Top 5 Coldest Monthly Lows in January
-------------------------------------
1) -34 degrees - 1975.
2) -30 degrees - 1989.
3) -28 degrees - 1972.
4) -27 degrees - 1973.
5) -24 degrees - 2009.

...Precipitation...

January 2025 was the 4th wettest January on record, with
1.69 inches of precipitation measured for the month. The
normal precipitation for January is 0.75 inches.

Top 5 Wettest Januarys on Record
--------------------------------
1) 2.71 inches - 1987.
2) 2.09 inches - 1963.
3) 1.71 inches - 2017.
4) 1.69 inches - 2025.***
5) 1.52 inches - 2014.

The wettest day in January was on the 6th, with 0.30 inches of
precipitation measured. The second wettest day in January was
on the 13th, with 0.22 inches of precipitation measured.

Number of days in January
-------------------------

Daily Precipitation of a trace or more - 18 days
Daily Precipitation of 0.01 inches (measurable) or more - 14 days
Daily Precipitation of 0.10 inches or more - 10 days
Daily Precipitation of 0.25 inches or more - 1 day


...Snowfall...

January 2025 was the 67th snowiest January on record, with 2.4
inches of snow measured for the month. The normal monthly snowfall
for January is 12.4 inches.

Top 5 Snowiest Januarys on Record
---------------------------------
1) 34.4 inches - 2000.
2) 31.7 inches - 2017.
3) 29.3 inches - 2007.
4) 27.5 inches - 1990.
5) 27.2 inches - 2008.

The snowiest day in January was on the 9th, with 1.4 inches of
snow measured. The second snowiest day in January was on the 27th,
with 0.3 inches of snow measured.

Number of days in January
-------------------------

Daily Snowfall of a trace or more - 10 days
Daily Snowfall of 0.1 inches (measurable) or more - 6 days
Daily Snowfall of 1.0 inches or more - 1 day


...Snow Depth...

The average snow depth for January 2025 was 4.3 inches. The
maximum snow depth was 8 inches last measured on the 6th. The
minimum snow depth was 0 inches on the 27th. The record
average snow depth for January is 26.9 inches, set in 1956.

Top 5 Maximum January Snow Depths
---------------------------------
1) 47 inches - 1956.
2) 37 inches - 2024.
2) 34 inches - 1992
3) 32 inches - 1960.
4) 31 inches - 1967.


...January 2025 vs. January 2024...

January 2025 was warmer and wetter than January 2024.

Temperature: 26.7 degrees in 2025 vs. 13.2 degrees in 2024.
Precipitation: 1.69 inches in 2025 vs. 1.29 inches in 2024.
Snowfall: 2.4" in 2025 vs. 25.5" in 2024.
